About me

I’m a freelance writer based in Austin, Texas with more than a decade of experience covering the complex dynamics of race, ethnicity, wealth, poverty and power and how they are shaping the future of Texas and Texans, in the long and short term.

I worked previously for The Texas Tribune from 2013 to 2023. My work has appeared in other publications, such as Texas Monthly Magazine, The New York Times, The Washington Post, Texas Highways, The Guardian, NPR, Preservation Magazine, and more.
